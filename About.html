About Us Page

Zen's Boutique was birthed by two very passionate
and vibrant partners,Zenande Dotwana and Sinazo Madam.
Both have an immense passion for beautiful things and making the world
a brighter,happier more fun filled place,a place filled with gorgeous,
high fashion for every woman!After many fun filled afternoons and much girl time,
Zen's Boutique combusted from their combined years of experience across many
fields of expertise,namely retail,customer service,business and fashion!
There was a clear gap in the South African market for beautifully fitted,
high fashion plus size clothing!
